Key Engineering Formulae

L = μ₀ × b × [ln(8b/a) − 2]

C = 1 / (ω² × L)

Rr = 31171 × (A / λ²)²

δ = 1 / √(π f μ₀ σ)

Rloss = Cloop / (π dtube σ δ)

η = Rr / (Rr + Rloss)

Q = ωL / (Rr + Rloss)

Vcap = √(P / Rtotal) × ωL
⚡
HIGH VOLTAGE WARNING: At 100 W, capacitor voltages exceed 6 700 V on 80 m and 5 500 V on 40 m. Mount the antenna out of reach (≥ 2.5 m above ground). Use motor-driven tuning only. Never touch the loop or capacitor during transmission. These voltages are lethal.
